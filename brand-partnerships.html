How it works for the consumers

We make shopping a rewarding experience through our consumer-facing app. We ensure that consumers are rewarded for their everyday shopping, and for their brand loyalty, across all retailers.

01

Shop anywhere

We reward our users with points for uploading any retail receipt. This can be from in-store shopping, or online.

02

Brand bonus

They get points for all their transactions, but they receive bonus points whenever they buy our partner brands.

03

Step bonus

To encourage a more active lifestyle, consumers are rewarded with even more points when they reach an average of 5k steps per day.

04

Get rewards

They can withdraw their points to their PayPal account, or they can choose to donate their earnings to a charity of their choice.
